About the assignments

The assignments were about creating a good and a bad map in R. The good map is well designed and functional, while the bad map is intentionally poorly designed to demonstrate what not to do in map creation.
The final project was about trying to prove the hypothesis that the craters on the moon are unevenly distributed in favour of the farside (more craters on the farside). This aligns with current scientific research which theorizes that one big impact is the main cause for that condition, enabling volcanic activity on the nearside and therefore covering older craters there.
