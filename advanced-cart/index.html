Vaccination rates across the world

The map shows the vaccination rate of various diseases across the world. Select a year and disease type to see how vaccination rates change across regions.

About this map

This interactive map visualizes the global vaccination coverage data of the 4 most notorious diseases in children preventable by vaccination:

  • BCG (bacillus calmette-guérin – also known as tuberculosis)
  • DTP3 (diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis)
  • MCV1 (measles-containing vaccine)
  • POL3 (polio-containing vaccine where children of 1 year of age received 3 doses)

Data source: WHO / UNICEF
